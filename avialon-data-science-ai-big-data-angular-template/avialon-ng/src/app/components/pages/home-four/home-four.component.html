Computer Vision Annotation

We deliver precise computer vision solutions with our expert image and video dataset annotation services. This human-guided task involves identifying, categorising, and drawing boundaries around specific elements, helping machines learn to interpret visual information accurately and consistently.

  • Bounding Boxes

    Our computer vision experts use bounding box annotation to detect and define object locations in images and videos. By carefully drawing rectangular frames around target objects, we enable machine learning algorithms to identify and track objects with precision.

  • Polygon Annotation

    Our expert annotators use polygon annotation to precisely outline objects with complex or irregular shapes. By plotting connected points along each vertex and edge, we create tight-fitting polygons that accurately capture the object’s exact silhouette.

  • Image Segmentation

    Our team specializes in image segmentation, a precise annotation method that partitions images into distinct segments at the pixel level. Each pixel is carefully assigned to predefined classes or categories, allowing for detailed identification of specific objects or areas of interest.

  • Keypoint Annotation

    Keypoint annotation involves precisely marking critical points of interest in images and videos, such as facial landmarks, body joints, or product features. Our annotators create detailed keypoint skeletons by connecting multiple points, enabling AI models to understand spatial relationships and object orientation.

3D Point Cloud Annotation

Point clouds, captured by sensors like LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging), represent data points in 3D space and are vital for applications like autonomous vehicles and robotics. Our team at Aya Data specializes in precise 3D point cloud and LiDAR annotation, labeling objects and scenes to create high quality datasets.

  • 3D Bounding Boxes

    3D bounding boxes encapsulate objects in 3D space, providing position, orientation, and dimensions. Our team precisely places these boxes around objects, crucial for tasks like autonomous driving.

  • 3D Polygon Annotation

    3D polygon annotation creates detailed outlines of objects in three-dimensional space. We trace complex shapes, ideal for applications requiring fine-grained object segmentation.

  • 3D Image Segmentation

    3D semantic segmentation assigns class labels to individual points or voxels in a 3D scene. Our annotators carefully categorise each element, enabling AI to understand complex 3D environments.

  • LiDAR Annotation

    LiDAR annotation involves labeling 3D data from LiDAR sensors or scans to identify objects like vehicles, pedestrians, buildings, and terrain. Our experts segment and classify point clouds, enabling accurate object recognition for applications in autonomous driving, robotics, and geospatial analysis.

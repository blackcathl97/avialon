3D Point Cloud Annotation

Point clouds, captured by sensors like LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging), represent data points in 3D space and are vital for applications like autonomous vehicles and robotics. Our team at Aya Data specializes in precise 3D point cloud and LiDAR annotation, labeling objects and scenes to create high quality datasets.

  • 3D Bounding Boxes

    3D bounding boxes encapsulate objects in 3D space, providing position, orientation, and dimensions. Our team precisely places these boxes around objects, crucial for tasks like autonomous driving.

  • 3D Polygon Annotation

    3D polygon annotation creates detailed outlines of objects in three-dimensional space. We trace complex shapes, ideal for applications requiring fine-grained object segmentation.

  • 3D Image Segmentation

    3D semantic segmentation assigns class labels to individual points or voxels in a 3D scene. Our annotators carefully categorise each element, enabling AI to understand complex 3D environments.

  • LiDAR Annotation

    LiDAR annotation involves labeling 3D data from LiDAR sensors or scans to identify objects like vehicles, pedestrians, buildings, and terrain. Our experts segment and classify point clouds, enabling accurate object recognition for applications in autonomous driving, robotics, and geospatial analysis.
